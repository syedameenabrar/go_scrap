How It Works

  1. Home
  2. Pages
  3. How It Works

01

Quote Acceptance

Car owner accepts the quote and sends us a copy of the RC and ID proof to verify there is no hypothecation (loan) or legal case on the car/bike.

02

Payment and Doc

Payment is made in cash or online, copy of RC is collected and affidavit will be given.

03

Car Pickup

We pick the ELV from any location in Delhi NCR and give the owner an Assurance Letter from our company.

04

RC De-Registration

Customer has to submit the application for RC de-registration to the RTO along with chassis and engine plate, original RC, authorized scrap letter.

05

Car Recycled

The car is de-polluted and dismantled in a clean and non-polluting manner. Salvageable parts are sold and the remaining parts and scrapped.

06

Certificate Handing

The De-registration certificate is obtained from RTO and handed to the owner along with an assurance letter from us.
